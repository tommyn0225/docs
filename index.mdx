## Get up and running

Follow these four steps to go from zero to a fully tracked collection.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for a free account">
    Head to the sign-up page and create your account using your email address and a password. No credit card or subscription is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a binder">
    From your dashboard, click **New Binder**, give it a name, and select the game it will hold — Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon, or One Piece TCG.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and add cards">
    Use the Search page to find cards by name, set, type, rarity, or price range, then add them to your binder with the quantity you own.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your collection value">
    Return to your dashboard to see your total collection value in USD, your overall card count, and a value-over-time chart that updates as market prices change.
  </Step>
</Steps>
