Binder is a free trading card collection tracker built for players, collectors, and traders who want a clear picture of what they own and what it's worth. Whether you're cataloguing a Commander staples binder, tracking your Pokémon pulls, or keeping tabs on your One Piece TCG investments, Binder gives you the tools to organize every card, follow live market prices, and share your collection with others — all without paying a subscription fee.

## Supported games

Binder currently supports three trading card games. Each binder is scoped to one game so that card data, pricing, and search filters stay accurate and relevant.

**Magic: The Gathering**
Search cards by name, set, type, rarity, color, and price range. Live market pricing reflects current secondary-market values for every card in your binder.

**Pokémon TCG**
Add Pokémon cards by name, set, and rarity. Market prices update automatically so your binder value stays current as the meta shifts.

**One Piece TCG**
Track your One Piece card collection the same way — search by name, set, and rarity, and let Binder handle the live pricing.

<Note>
  Binder is completely free to use. There is no subscription, no premium tier, and no credit card required — ever.
</Note>
