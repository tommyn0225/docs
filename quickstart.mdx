This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a brand-new account to a live, price-tracked card collection. Each step takes only a moment, and you'll have your first binder up and running before you know it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the sign-up page and enter your email address and a password. Binder doesn't require a credit card or a subscription — your account is free the moment you sign up. After submitting the form you'll land directly on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first binder">
    From your dashboard, click **New Binder**. Give your binder a name — something like "Modern Staples" or "Base Set Collection" — then select the game it will hold: **Magic: The Gathering**, **Pokémon**, or **One Piece TCG**. Click **Create** to save it. You can create up to three binders on your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for cards">
    Open the Search page to find cards to add to your binder. You can filter by:

    - **Name** — type any part of a card's name
    - **Set** — narrow results to a specific expansion or base set
    - **Type** — filter by card type (e.g. Creature, Trainer, Character)
    - **Rarity** — Common, Uncommon, Rare, and beyond
    - **Color** — for Magic: The Gathering cards
    - **Price range** — set a minimum and maximum market price to find cards within a budget

    Browse the results to find the cards you own.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add cards to your binder">
    From any card in the search results, click **Add to Binder**. Select the binder you want to add the card to, enter the quantity you own, and confirm. Repeat this for every card in your collection. You can always come back to update quantities or add more cards later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your collection value">
    Return to your dashboard. You'll see your **total collection value** in USD, your **total card count**, and a **value-over-time chart** that updates automatically as market prices change. Click into any binder from the dashboard to see a detailed breakdown of every card it contains, along with each card's individual market price.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can create up to 3 binders per account. For the cleanest experience, we recommend dedicating one binder to each game — one for Magic: The Gathering, one for Pokémon, and one for One Piece TCG — so your pricing data and search filters stay focused.
</Tip>
