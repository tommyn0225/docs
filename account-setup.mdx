Your Binder account is the home for all of your binders, card holdings, and collection data. This page covers how to create and sign in to your account, adjust your display preferences, and manage your profile — so you can spend less time on setup and more time tracking cards.

<Info>
  Binder uses email and password authentication. No third-party social login (such as Google or GitHub) is required or supported — just a valid email address and a password of your choosing.
</Info>

## Create an account

Setting up a new Binder account takes less than a minute and is completely free.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Navigate to the sign-up page in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type in your email address and choose a password of at least 6 characters. Using a strong, unique password is always a good idea.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Click **Sign Up** to create your account. You'll be taken directly to your dashboard, where you can create your first binder and start adding cards right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in to your account

If you already have an account, sign in to pick up right where you left off.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Navigate to the login page in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter the email address and password you used when you signed up, then click **Sign In**. You'll be taken to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adjust your display settings

Binder includes a display preference you can change at any time from the Settings page.

**Dark / Light mode**
Toggle between dark mode and light mode to suit your environment and personal preference. Your selection is saved to your account and persists across sessions and devices.

More settings options are on the way — check back for updates as Binder continues to grow.

## Manage your profile

Your profile page lets you review your account information and sign out of Binder.

**View account info**
Open your profile to see the email address associated with your account.

**Sign out**
Click the **Sign Out** button on your profile page to end your current session. You can sign back in at any time from the login page.
