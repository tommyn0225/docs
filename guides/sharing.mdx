Binder lets you share individual binders and your trade list with anyone — even people who don't have a Binder account. Each shared page is a clean, read-only view that shows your cards, quantities, and prices. Sharing is controlled by a simple public/private toggle, and you can turn it off at any moment.

## Sharing a Binder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the binder">
    From your Dashboard, click the binder you want to share to open its detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Binder Settings">
    Click the **Settings** icon or link on the binder detail page to open binder settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle 'Public'">
    Switch the **Public** toggle to the on position. The binder is now accessible to anyone with the link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the URL and share it">
    Your binder's public URL is `/b/{binder-id}`. Copy it from the settings panel or directly from your browser's address bar after toggling on, then send it to whoever you'd like to share your collection with.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When someone visits your public binder they can see:

- All of your holdings with **card images** and **card names**.
- The **quantity** you own of each card.
- Current **prices** for each card.
- The **total collection value** of the binder.

To stop sharing, return to Binder Settings and toggle **Public** back to off. The URL immediately becomes inaccessible to anyone who isn't signed in as you.

## Sharing Your Trade List

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to /trades">
    Navigate to your trade list at `/trades`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle 'Share trade list'">
    Switch the **Share trade list** toggle to the on position to generate a public link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the URL and share it">
    Your trade list's public URL is `/t/{token}`. Copy it from the toggle area and send it to prospective trading partners.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When someone visits your public trade list they can see:

- All cards you've marked 'for trade', with **card names**, **images**, and **quantities**.

## Privacy

<Note>
  All binders and your trade list are **private by default**. Nothing is visible publicly until you explicitly toggle it on.
</Note>

Keep the following in mind as you manage sharing:

- **Binders** — toggling a binder back to private instantly removes public access. The same binder ID is used if you make it public again, so any previously shared link will work once you re-enable it.
- **Trade list** — the share token is permanent and tied to your account. Toggling sharing off stops all access, but the same token is reused when you turn sharing back on. Links you've already shared will start working again automatically.
- **Viewers** — nobody needs a Binder account to view a shared binder or trade list. They only need the URL.
