Binders are your primary way to organize cards in Binder. Each binder belongs to a single game — Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon, or One Piece TCG — so your collections stay cleanly separated. From any binder's detail page you can track holdings, monitor total value over time, and mark cards you're willing to trade.

## Creating a Binder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Dashboard">
    Sign in and navigate to your Dashboard. Any binders you've already created are listed here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Binder'">
    Select the **New Binder** button to open the creation dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a name">
    Type a name for your binder. Choose something descriptive, such as "Modern Staples" or "Base Set Collection."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a game">
    Choose one of the supported games: **Magic: The Gathering**, **Pokémon**, or **One Piece TCG**. This determines which cards you can add and which price data is displayed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Create**. Your new binder appears on the Dashboard and is ready to fill with cards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can have a maximum of **3 binders** per account. If you've reached the limit, delete an existing binder before creating a new one.
</Note>

## Viewing a Binder

From your Dashboard, click the name or thumbnail of any binder to open its detail page at `/binders/[id]`. The detail page shows:

- Your full **holdings list** — every card you've added, along with quantities and current prices.
- A **value chart** tracking the total estimated worth of the binder over time.

## Editing a Holding

You can update any card already in your binder directly from the binder detail page.

1. Locate the card in your holdings list.
2. Click the **edit** icon (or the card row) to open the holding editor.
3. Adjust the **quantity** to reflect how many copies you own.
4. Toggle **For Trade** on or off to flag whether you're willing to trade that card.
5. Save your changes.

## Removing a Card

To remove a card from a binder, open the holding editor and select **Delete Holding**. This removes the card only from the current binder — it has no effect on the same card in any of your other binders.

## Renaming a Binder

1. Open the binder detail page.
2. Navigate to **Binder Settings** (gear icon or settings link).
3. Edit the **Name** field and save.

The new name is reflected on your Dashboard immediately.

## Deleting a Binder

You can delete a binder from **Binder Settings** on the detail page.

<Warning>
  Deleting a binder is permanent and cannot be undone. All holdings inside the binder are removed at the same time — the cards are not transferred anywhere else. Make sure you no longer need the binder or its holdings before confirming the deletion.
</Warning>
